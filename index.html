About the Project



The concept of Answer Pi Box is very similar to tarot cards.
Ask a question to the Raspberry Pi and shake it.
As you shake the raspberry pi, the icon on the screen
will appear and it will give you solution or an insight
if it's good or bad.

Materials Needed
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Sense HAT
  • HDMI Cable
  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
